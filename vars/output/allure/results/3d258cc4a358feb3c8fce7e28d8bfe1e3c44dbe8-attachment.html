San Diego Comic Con is being held from the comfort of your own home this year, and to coincide with this special cyber-con, Popcultcha will be releasing a rad line-up of @originalfunko 2020 Summer Convention Exclusive items! Dropping online and at our Collector Gallery at 2:01PM Thursday 23rd July AEST. Here’s what you need to know about the launch... Online: We’ll be bringing our website down at 12:00PM AEST on the day to prepare the site. We’ll have our trusty queue in place for the event, and at 12:00PM the pre-queue will kick off. If you’re in this pre-queue before 2:00PM you’ll be randomly assigned a place in line once the website re-opens, however if you enter the queue after 2:00PM then you’ll be at the back of the line. Be sure to jump in the pre-queue for the best possible position. Quantity limits will be in place. Be sure to check out the Convention Release FAQ to be up to speed for the event! You can find it here: bit.ly/convention-exclusive-faq. In Store: For the in-store launch, we’ll ONLY be releasing the 2020 Summer Convention Exclusives at the Popcultcha Collector Gallery in North Geelong due to the larger space that is available there available there and our ability to control social distancing more effectively. These exclusives will release at 2:01PM, to coincide with the online release. We’ll have strict measures in place to ensure customers are social distancing, and there will be a cap on the people allowed in store at any time. This is to ensure the safety of our customers and our staff during these challenging times. We encourage any collectors in regional Victoria to come along, however, we kindly request that any residents of Metro Melbourne and Mitchell Shire please do not attend due to the Stage 3 lockdown of those areas. We are sorry for this inconvenience. If you do live in a Stage 3 Lockdown Area, our product offering will be available for you to purchase online only. We will reserve the right to refuse entry to those who are not social distancing or who we know have come down to Geelong from a Stage 3 Lockdown Area in Melbourne. Quantity limits will be in place. #popcultcha #2020summerconventionexclusive #funkopop
